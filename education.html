Journey of Education

Education shapes our lives from early childhood to advanced professional certifications. Here is a general overview of the educational journey globally.

Stage Description Typical Age / Duration
Kindergarten / Pre-school Introduction to social skills, basic numeracy and literacy. 3–6 years
Primary / Elementary School Basic education in reading, writing, arithmetic, science, and social studies. 6–12 years
Secondary / High School Intermediate education including sciences, arts, and languages. Prepares students for higher education. 12–18 years
Undergraduate / Bachelor's Degree Specialized higher education in a chosen field. Globally recognized degree. 18–22 years
Postgraduate / Master's Degree Advanced study focusing on research and expertise in a specialized area. 22–25 years
Doctorate / PhD Highest academic qualification focusing on original research and contributions to a field. 23+ years
Professional Certificates & Diplomas Short-term specialized courses like IT certifications, language diplomas, project management, etc. Varies
Continuing Education / Online Learning Lifelong learning through workshops, MOOCs, online courses, and seminars. Any age
